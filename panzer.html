War Horse of Deutschland

3D model animation video

About

This video is a documentary that provide a brief introduction about the German Panzer IV tank in WW2, which majorly described how the tank was built and the improvements has been made during the battlefield. The video would demonstrate how German tanks grow from "tiny five ton packages to one hundred-ton monsters."

Narrative & Storyboard

As our draft storyboard presented, the tank will be modelled and animated in a bottom-up procedure. We starts with continuous track to armour and gun, including interiors. As the modelled tank transforms in our video, corresponded documentaries and footages would strengthen and expand the content narration.

Model & Animatic

My major role on this project is model making and animation. After the first version of our tank was completed, I started to work on the component and camera moving by viewing the storyboard.

Environment & Final Render

Due to the calculation speed of our computer, we have to finalize the animation first and then build environment and texture on the model. Rendering also took huge amount of time, personally, this project did not reach my highest satisfaction, but I indeed learned a lot of experience from it.
